Olle Blomberg

I completed a PhD in Philosophy at the University of Edinburgh in 2013, in the School of Philosophy, Psychology and Language Sciences. My thesis is concerned with philosophical issues about cooperation and social cognition. It is entitled Joint action without and beyond planning.

My research interests are mainly in the philosophy of mind and action, and in the philosophy of cognitive science and social science. I also have a keen interest in empirical work on joint action and social cognition.
